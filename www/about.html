IAMA...

I'm a programmer, currently living and working in Sydney, Australia but originally from Dundee, Scotland. My first paid role was an IT tech, but I quickly moved into web development after I graduated from my Computing degree. Whilst being a PHP programmer is far from glamorous, at the time it was - and to a point still is - a great language for quickly building websites.

I started experimenting with Ruby and Rails for some side-projects back in 2007, and after leaving Yahoo! in 2009 I took a full-time Rails develpoment role. I have a lot of fun coding in Rails, but am developing a soft-spot for Haskell. Time will tell what my next move is.

Outside of nerdy things, I love spending time playing with my sons, and relaxing with my wife, generally not at the same time. I love football (soccer) and whilst a pretty mediocre player, will turn up to play come rain, hail or sun. I also really enjoy films, particuarly sci-fi (of course) and abstract work that makes me think.
