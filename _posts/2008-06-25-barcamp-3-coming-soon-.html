Hello Campers!

It’s that time of the year again – BarCampSydney3 is almost upon us! This year it’s hosted at the Roundhouse at UNSW on Anzac Parade, and as ever there’s “No spectators, only participants”.

Be prepared to get involved with BarCamp, everyone has something to give and BarCamp gives you an ideal forum to find other like-minded geeks to bounce your thoughts and ideas off.

You can get more info regarding the BarCamp concept, transport to the venue, and much much more at http://www.barcampsydney.org/
